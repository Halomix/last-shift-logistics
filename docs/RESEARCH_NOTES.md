# Research Notes

## Roblox Discovery And Return-Value
Primary source used in this sprint:
- [Roblox Creator Hub - Discovery](https://create.roblox.com/docs/discovery)

Applied insights:
- discovery favors stronger engagement, repeat play, and session quality
- return-value matters, so the game should reward repeated runs instead of feeling solved in one shift
- events, boards, notifications, and visible community signals matter for reactivation
- monetization should stay fair and should not replace real loop quality

What changed because of this:
- rotating offer boards
- featured-district incentives
- dispatch streak bonuses
- stronger company-rank and mastery feedback
- world-life presence to make the city feel active

## Trucking / Logistics Presentation
Research already logged in `docs/RESEARCH_LOG.md` continued to support:
- compact driving HUD
- richer job context in briefing/summary
- industrial delivery-space composition
- stronger sense of machine weight and route consequence

What changed because of this:
- multi-stop jobs kept the live HUD compact but made the summary richer
- ambient actors and yard motion were added instead of more text explanation
