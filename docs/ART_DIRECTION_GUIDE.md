# Art Direction Guide

## Visual Direction
The target look is a **stylized, social, night logistics city** with clean Roblox-native forms and strong readability.

## Emotional Goals
- useful
- known
- busy
- returnable
- aspirational
- slightly funny without becoming parody

## Shared Depot Direction
- the depot should feel like the player’s public home base
- it should be brighter, more welcoming, and more legible than route spaces
- players should immediately understand:
  - where to get jobs
  - where trucks are
  - where status is shown
  - where routes leave the hub

## District Art Notes
- **Market Nine**
  - commercial awnings
  - stacked signs
  - tighter lane geometry
- **Floodline**
  - barriers
  - caution lamps
  - damp infrastructure
- **Dockside Ring**
  - containers
  - gantries
  - freight lots
- **Brightline Civic**
  - gates
  - permit signage
  - orderly civic loading spaces
- **Old Transit**
  - relay yards
  - rail remnants
  - shadowed service structures

## Color Strategy
- hub uses welcoming steel-blue / amber utility tones
- districts use distinct accent families
- public signage and navigation cues use strong accent contrast

## Lighting Strategy
- night-first
- brighter pool lighting in the hub
- stronger contrast and mood away from the hub
- readable path lighting at route exits and delivery compounds

## Material Strategy
- concrete
- asphalt
- painted metal
- utility plastic
- neon / emissive signage and work lights

## UI Direction
- route-advisor clarity
- compact HUD
- bold headers
- readable badges and icons
- stronger board hierarchy than debug text
